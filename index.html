Dogs

American Staffordshire Terrier

The American Staffordshire Terrier is a breed of dog bred in the United States.

Belgian Shepherd

The Belgian Shepherd is a breed of dog. They belong to the shepherd breeds.

Doberman

Doberman is a breed of short-haired service dog, bred in the town of Apolda in the late 19th century by Carl Friedrich Louis Doberman.

French Bulldog

The French Bulldog is a breed of dog. Small, characterized by a large but short muzzle, flat bifurcated nose, wide bifurcated upper lip.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd Dog is a breed of dog originally used as a herding and service dog.

Labrador-Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a breed of dog. It was originally bred as a hunting dog. Labradors are used for hunting, as guide dogs, rescue dogs, but mainly as companions.
